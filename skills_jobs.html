Skills and Jobs

If our project were to expand going forward, there would be several important roles that would likely need to be filled. For example Android Software Engineer, Cyber Security Analyst, Project Manager, and IT Systems Manager

About Us

In Control, Totally is a Sydney-based company, with employees in multiple states and territories. We are looking for a full-time Full Stack Android Software Engineer to join our development team.

The sole product that we deliver is Cyclopedia. Our vision with Cyclopedia is to aid the uptake of commuter and everyday cycling by empowering and informing those who ride, bringing together the cycling community, and aiding local governments with maintenance reports. We are committed to helping cyclists in many ways, and our goal is to build the cycling community, as well as aiding and encouraging others to do the same.

We have employees working in person and remotely, and supporting our employees is very important to us. Whether you come into the office every day or only once a month, you will fit in well with our friendly and supportive team. We run a very flexible workflow, and willingly offer extra time off if and when you need it.

Full Stack Android Software Engineer

In this role, you will be joining our small but motivated development team in the further development and expansion of Cyclopedia. In particular, you will be working on the frontend Android Application, while also ensuring that the backend connectivity is working smoothly.

As this job is working predominantly with Android development, at least 3 years of experience with Kotlin/Java development is what we’re after. Ideally, you’d also need to have a good idea of what you're doing with the Google Cloud Platform.

As with many jobs, a Bachelor of Computer Science, Bachelor of Information Technology or similar is a definite plus, but we realize that many developers have amazing talent and skill that hasn’t been formalised, and we’re more than happy to take applications with no formal qualification at all!

TThe last thing we’re looking for is experience using and integrating REST APIs. This is an important part of our project because we run maintenance reports for government departments and the like, so it would be great if you have a bit of know-how in this area.

Here are a few of the things you’ll end up doing in this position:

  • Developing and testing our Android application (using predominantly Kotlin and Java).
  • Improving and maintaining our existing Android codebase.
  • Integrating the app with our backend (Including Google Cloud Platform) and other relevant APIs such as Google Maps and Apple MapKit, and our own internal and client-facing APIs
  • Ensuring our services are functional and performant.
  • Working with our helpful security team to provide maximum security and uptime for our users and other stakeholders.
  • Working and cooperating with our team to provide the best possible experience and services to our users and employees alike.

Cyber Security Analyst

In this role, you will be analysing the security of our back and frontend products including Google Cloud Platform, Android and iOS apps, and webpages, and communicating your findings with our teams. Cybersecurity is an extremely important matter, and at In Control, Totally, we are dedicated to giving our customers and stakeholders the best and most secure experience, with minimal downtime (or any other inconveniences for that matter!).

As you will be filling an essential role in our team, tertiary study in Cyber Security is desirable. For example, Bachelor of Cyber Security, Bachelor of Computer Science with a major in Cyber Security, or Bachelor in IT with a postgraduate diploma in Cyber Security. that being said, however, we are extremely willing to consider applicants with other adjacent study or work experience. We’d be more than happy, for example, to hire someone with 4 years of relevant industry experience.

Here's a little list with some of the things you’ll be doing in this role:

  • Analysing and reviewing the security measures within the backend and frontend parts of our projects
  • Checking that new releases and updates are working securely according to our standards
  • Integrating the app with our backend (Including Google Cloud Platform) and other relevant APIs such as Google Maps and Apple MapKit, and our own internal and client-facing APIs
  • Ensuring all our internal and client-facing APIs are secure and functional.
  • Working actively with our teams to ensure the best possible experience for our users and stakeholders.

Project Manager

In this role, you will be overseeing our product development team and assisting with the completion and maintenance of our main product, Cyclopedia. You will also be responsible for the day-to-day management and ensuring scheduling, scope and quality requirements are met.

For this role qualifications in Project Management or Bachelor of Business (Management) would be preferred however we would also consider applicants that have a proven track record leading a small team.

Some of the main responsibilities of this role would be:

  • Managing project objectives, project scope, roles & responsibilities
  • Tracking and maintaining costs to ensure the product is delivered within budget.
  • Developing a project plan and scheduling key milestones.
  • Identifying and alleviating potential risks.
  • Managing a team of 6 to 10 people.

IT Systems Manager

In this role, you will be responsible for the security, integrity, and performance of our computer systems. You will also be responsible for the maintenance of our hardware and ensuring our systems are up to date and functional.

A bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or Project Management would be ideal for this role, however, we understand that on-the-job experience can be just as good as a degree and are willing to consider applicants with at least 3 years of experience in a similar role.

Some of the main responsibilities of this role would be:

  • Evaluating the functionality of systems and updating as required. /li>
  • Managing and maintaining hardware and software
  • Testing and modifying systems to ensure that that they operate reliably
  • Managing networking systems to ensure on site and remote access
  • Safeguard assets by planning and implementing disaster recovery and back-up procedures.
